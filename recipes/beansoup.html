Bean Soup

Description

This recipe makes a large pot worth of Portuguese bean soup. Delicious and wonderful to have on a cold day. Also a great way to get your vegetables!

Ingredients

  • 4 Cans of Romano Beans
  • 1 Small Tomato Paste Can
  • 1 Medium Dimpled Cabbage
  • 2 Big Carrots
  • 6 - 8 Large Yukon Gold Potatoes
  • Â¼ Cup Olive Oil
  • Medium Onion (Quartered)

Instructions

  1. Fill large stock pot 1/3 full of water.
  2. Add cut up ingredients before boiling.
  3. Set heat to high until boiling. Once boiling turn down to medium.
  4. Cook until water thickens (multiple hours).
